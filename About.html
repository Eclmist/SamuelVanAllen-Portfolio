A little background




Hi! I'm Samuel

I'm a final year student in Singapore Polytechnic studying Games development as part of my diploma course. I have been making games since 2013.


I'm a aspiring graphics programmer

I strongly believe that good visuals are one of the most crucial elements of video games; It has always been my top priority during development. Having worked mostly with Unity in the past, I soon realised that its basic functionalities was insufficient, and could not produce many of the visual effects that I was looking for. My search for real-time rendering techniques and special effects led me into the world of graphics programming.

I'm someone who works well with concepts, regardless of the tool. This website, for instance, was written when I had little javascript or CSS background, but was possible because concepts from games development easily carried over.
