A little background



My name is Samuel.

I am a second year student (2016) in Singapore Polytechnic, studying Games development as part of my diploma course. I have been making games for close to 6 years now, with experiences in Unity 5, Unreal, OpenGL, XNA and DirectX.

I strongly believe that good visuals are one of the most crucial elements of video games. Making my games look good was always my top priority during development. Having worked mostly with Unity in the past, I soon realised that its basic functionalities was insufficient, and could not provide many of the visual effects that I was looking for. My search for high fidelity rendering and special effects led me into the world of graphics programming.
