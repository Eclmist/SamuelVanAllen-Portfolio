PROJECTS

Some of my past projects are featured below

Wither

Scheduled to be released somewhere in 2017, Wither is a unique fantasy role playing game with a heavy emphasis on its narrative, puzzle solving as well as real time combat.

The player will step into the shoes of an unnamed boy who, with the help of his companion Ivy, will take on a mixture of puzzles and face many enemies throughout his journey to bring colour back into a monochromatic world.

Graphics is one of the main focuses of Wither. The partially monochromatic setting of the game world and the allowance of interaction with colours in Wither is achieved with many screen spaced image effects written in ShaderLab and CG.

Role: Project Lead, Graphics Programmer

V3

V3 is a 2 player co-op rouguelike bullet-hell game.

Made during a 30 hour game jam at James Cook University, it was highly inspired by the game "Geometry Wars", V3 was our attempt to create a game that looked visually stunning, yet fit the retro theme of the game jam.

Some notable effects implemented in V3 includes a bullet wavefront effect - essentially a multithreaded cloth simulation, as well as a couple of image effects.

Role: Graphics Programmer, Gameplay Programmer

Valenwood

This is not a Bethesda game.

Valenwood was made as part of a assignment at Singapore Polytechnic. Many elements such as the HUD and sound were borrowed from Skyrim.

As much as Valenwood was a assignment, it was also a personal challenge to see to what extend can one replicate the graphical fidelity of a AAA title with little time and resource.

Role: N.A (Solo project)

Borealis Laboratories

This is not a Valve game.

Borealis Laboratories a level design demo, created using Unreal. Everything from 3D modeling to animations were all made completely from scratch (yes, I didn't steal the GlaDOS).

You can download the playable build here if you want to take a closer look.

Role: N.A (Solo project)
