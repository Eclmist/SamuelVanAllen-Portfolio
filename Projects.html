Wither

Scheduled to be released somewhere in 2017, Wither is a unique fantasy role playing game with a heavy emphasis on its narrative, puzzle solving as well as real time combat.

The player will step into the shoes of an unnamed boy who, with the help of his companion Ivy, will take on a mixture of puzzles and face many enemies throughout his journey to bring colour back into a monochromatic world.

Graphics is one of the main focuses of Wither. The partially monochromatic setting of the game world and the allowance of interaction with colours in Wither is achieved with many screen spaced image effects written in ShaderLab and CG.

Role: Project Lead, Graphics Programmer
