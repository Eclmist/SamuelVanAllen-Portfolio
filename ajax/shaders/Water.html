Water

Made with Unreal Engine's material editor

This custom water material is created using a combination of the following:

  • A scrolling normal map for waves
  • Fresnel for both water color and reflection
  • Vertex offset with using world space position and sine function
  • Depth fade for both color and reflections

This was considerably one of the easier materials to create, considering unreal's material nodes handled most of the logic (built in fresnel and depth calculations, etc).
