Reveal

Made as part of the game Aether.

Inspired by the similar visual effect in Beyond Eyes, this effect reveals the environment wherever deemed appropriate by design. This method is slightly different in that we make use of a 3D texture as a visibility mask and a compute shader to update the mask texels, rather than through the more inefficient approach of updating vertex colors directly.

There are two main components to this effect.

  • A 3D texture that is dynamically updated by a compute shader
  • Materials that sample the 3D texture as a mask for visibility.

The rest is a little bit of tech art (glowing, flowing edges with noise and color ramp, etc.)
