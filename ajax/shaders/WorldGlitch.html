World Glitch

Made as part of the game Snowfield.

This is a 3 part effect involving a replacement shader, screen space distortions, as well as chromatic abberation.

Steps to render involves:

  • Render scene to a seperate buffer with a replacement shader (purple flickering squares)
  • Distort the rendered image with fractal noise and composit purple squares to it
  • Animate chromatic abberation in script.
