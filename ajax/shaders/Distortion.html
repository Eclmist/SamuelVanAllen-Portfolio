Distortion Sphere

Made as part of the game Wither.

This fragment shader produces a distorted sphere effect.

Steps to render involves:

  • Render intersection highlights by sampling depth texture and comparing against fragment depth
  • Animate fragment UV against time, to scroll a normal map across the sphere. THe sphere can be specially UV mapped to create different scrolling effects
  • Render texture behind the sphere using Grab Pass with offset'ed uvs to produce distortion effect
