Ether

Ether is a 3D renderer written with DirectX 12 and C++, meant to be used as a platform for creating and testing modern graphics rendering techniques.

Some of the features Ether supports are:

  • Deferred renderer with UE4 microfacet BRDF and image based lighting
  • DirectX12 objects/memory management (descriptors/heap allocators, PSO caching, etc.)
  • Supports live shader editing and hot reloading
  • Connection to WPF editor with data driven components over TCP
