The Room

Unity can look amazing.

This fully VR ready room was created with photorealism in mind. Most artifacts associated with the "Unity" look has been eliminated.

This was done by accurately and carefully tweeking PBR materials, utilizing Enlighten for realtime psuedo ray traced lights with baked emmisive maps with light/reflection probles for non-static objects, and Playdead's temporal AA to eliminate Unity's harsh edge look. Dithering was also used to eliminate colorbanding caused by Unity's post process stack which uses LDR buffers.
