Vectortree (2016)

Vectortree is a 2 player co-op rouguelike bullet-hell game.

Made during a 30 hour game jam at James Cook University, it was highly inspired by the game "Geometry Wars", V3 was our attempt to create a game that looked visually stunning, yet fit the retro theme of the game jam.

Some notable effects implemented in V3 includes a bullet wavefront effect - essentially a multithreaded cloth simulation, as well as a couple of image effects.

Time Taken: 30 Hours

Role: Graphics Programmer, Gameplay Programmer
