Fantasy Village (2017)

Fantasy Village is a recreation of one of the popular towns in the Bethesda game Skyrim, Riverwood.

As part of an effort to understand PBR and the Unreal Material Editor, the primary goal of this project was to create a level from scratch and getting it to look as good as possible.

Everything in this project - except texture and skybox - is created from scratch. This includes all models, materials, water, UE4 blueprints and particle systems.

Time Taken: 2 Weeks

Role: N.A (Pretty much solo work)
